# Concepts Hub

The knowledge layer. Frameworks and definitions that the rest of the vault depends on. These notes are mostly niche-agnostic — they hold true regardless of the active business-type overlay, with the exception of the E-E-A-T concept whose filename is parameterized.

## Frameworks

- [[FLOW Framework]] — Find / Leverage / Optimize / Win. The strategic backbone.
- [[HCU Recovery Framework]] — six-step recovery model for sites demoted by Google's Helpful Content System (or any algorithmic quality demotion).
- [[E-E-A-T for {{site_type}}]] — Experience / Expertise / Authoritativeness / Trust applied to the active vertical.
- [[Topical Authority for Niche Sites]] — hub-and-spoke architecture and coverage gap audit.
- [[Information Gain]] — the bar every refreshed and new page must clear.
- [[Content Pruning and Consolidation]] — when and how to remove or merge weak pages.
- [[Seasonal Search Demand]] — refresh and publish calendar for niches with cyclical demand.

## Limits and Anti-patterns

## How to Use This Hub

When a flow or decision references a concept, open the concept first to anchor the language. Concepts are the dictionary the rest of the vault uses — keep them open in a tab during sprint work.
